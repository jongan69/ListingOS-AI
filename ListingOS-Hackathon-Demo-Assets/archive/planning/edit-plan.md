# ListingOS Edit Plan

## Deliverables

- `listingos-devpost-30s-v1.mp4`
- `listingos-devpost-60s-v1.mp4`
- `listingos-devpost-90s-v1.mp4`
- `listingos-social-vertical-v1.mp4` if time and footage allow

## 30 Second Cut

Goal: instant impact and memorability.

Structure:

1. Hook: `Listing products online should not take 20 minutes per item.`
2. Brand: `ListingOS`
3. Photos in
4. AI draft generation
5. Review screen highlights
6. Listing-ready ending

## 60 Second Cut

Goal: primary Devpost version.

Structure:

1. Problem
2. Home / brand / seller context
3. Photo intake
4. AI processing and queue
5. Review screen walkthrough
6. Market/pricing/confidence proof
7. Listing-ready or published proof
8. Close on brand

## 90 Second Cut

Goal: extended proof-heavy version.

Structure:

1. Problem and category context
2. Home flow
3. Photo intake
4. Queue behavior
5. Deeper review walkthrough
6. Confidence / pricing / specifics
7. Verification / readiness
8. Publish or published proof
9. Strong branded ending

## Motion / Mockup Usage

- Rotato hero clip should lead the 30 second and 60 second versions.
- Raw in-app proof should dominate the middle of each cut.
- Rotato can return for the final brand close if it improves polish without obscuring readability.

## Caption Style

- Short, high-contrast, sentence-case captions
- One idea at a time
- Avoid dense paragraphs
- Match cuts to spoken beats

## Audio Direction

- Subtle premium electronic or modern house bed
- No distracting vocals
- Soft risers or impact hits only where they help transitions
- Keep the app itself as the visual focus

## Truth Rules

- No claim should overstate what the footage proves.
- Do not imply instant listing if the footage actually shows a wait.
- Do not imply a fresh publish if using stored published proof.
- Show only what can withstand scrutiny from judges.
