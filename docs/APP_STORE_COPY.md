## Store Description

ListingOS helps eBay sellers turn product photos into complete listings without rebuilding every item from scratch.

Take or select photos of one product, choose whether you want to sell faster or maximize profit, and let ListingOS build the draft. The app uses AI and marketplace context to generate a title, category, condition notes, item specifics, description, pricing strategy, and publish-readiness checks.

Shipped flow:

- Capture photos.
- Draft generation runs in the background while blocking checks are prepared.
- Review blockers, policy items, and pricing on one screen.
- Publish a fixed-price eBay listing only after seller review.

What ListingOS does:

- Creates listing drafts from product photos
- Writes buyer-ready eBay titles and descriptions
- Suggests category, condition, and item specifics
- Recommends pricing for fast sale, balanced sale, or max profit
- Checks seller readiness and required eBay fields
- Surfaces blockers in-app instead of hiding them in error messages
- Publishes fixed-price listings from one review screen
- Adds stricter identity and pricing safeguards for graded trading cards

ListingOS is built for sellers who move real inventory: resellers, collectors, side hustlers, card shops, thrift sellers, and anyone who wants to spend less time filling forms.

The app is intentionally simple. AI does the heavy lifting. You only step in when a marketplace rule, low-confidence identification, or seller preference truly needs a human decision.

Important: ListingOS is an independent seller tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by eBay. Publishing with production credentials can create live eBay listings.

## Review Notes Template

ListingOS uses eBay OAuth for seller sign-in. The demo build can be tested with a configured seller account and the deployed Cloudflare Worker. Publishing creates a real fixed-price eBay listing when production credentials are enabled, so reviewers should use the provided demo video or a sandbox-configured backend unless they intend to publish a real listing.

## External Voice Rules

- Lead with `Photos in. Listing out.`
- Describe the app as camera-first, seller-first, and review-first.
- Say `fixed-price eBay publishing` rather than implying full auction support.
- Say `active eBay comparables` rather than implying sold-comps support.
- Say `Android notifications verified` and keep iOS notification language qualified until device delivery is re-verified.

## Compliance Notes

- Do not claim affiliation with eBay.
- Do not claim sold-comps pricing until sold-comps support is live.
- Do not promise guaranteed sale speed.
- Do not imply AI can identify every item without review.
- Do not show private seller tokens, OAuth codes, API keys, or `.env` files.
